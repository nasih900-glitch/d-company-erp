# D Company Legal Documents Checklist

Prepared for: D Company ERP / cafe operations in Kerala, India
Review date: 2026-06-15
Purpose: complete document pack checklist for opening and running the company.

This is a document-control checklist. It tells you what legal papers to collect, create, sign, store, renew, and show during inspections. Final applicability must be confirmed by your CA, local licence consultant, and advocate.

## File Storage Rule

Create one master folder named:

`D_COMPANY_LEGAL_DOCUMENTS`

Inside it, create these folders:

- `01_entity_and_owners`
- `02_premises_and_property`
- `03_tax_and_accounting`
- `04_food_fssai`
- `05_local_licences`
- `06_fire_safety_building`
- `07_labour_hr_payroll`
- `08_environment_waste`
- `09_vendor_contracts`
- `10_customer_and_data`
- `11_events_music_gaming_hookah`
- `12_insurance`
- `13_erp_pos_audit`
- `14_returns_renewals_challans`
- `15_inspection_ready_pack`

For every document keep:

- PDF scan
- Original location
- Document number
- Issue date
- Expiry date
- Renewal reminder date
- Person responsible
- CA/consultant/advocate notes if any

## Red-Flag Documents You Must Not Ignore

Do not operate these activities without documents:

- [ ] Food service without FSSAI.
- [ ] Cafe/restaurant without local trade licence.
- [ ] Employees without labour registration/payroll records.
- [ ] GST invoices without correct GST setup.
- [ ] Public music without commercial licence if applicable.
- [ ] Ticketed screenings without content rights/licence.
- [ ] Hookah/tobacco without written local legal clearance.
- [ ] Fire-risk kitchen without extinguishers and safety records.
- [ ] Aggregator delivery without settlement/tax reconciliation.
- [ ] Staff sharing owner passwords.

D Company ERP Support

Support for staff and owners using the D Company ERP iPhone/iPad app.

Need help?

Email the D Company support address configured for your deployment with your name, device model, app version, login email, and a short description of the issue.

Before You Contact Support

  • Confirm the device has internet access.
  • Confirm your ERP account is active and your role has permission for the screen you are opening.
  • Sign out and sign in again if your session has expired.
  • For POS problems, write down the table/order/customer details before retrying.

Business-Critical Issues

For billing, GST, order, payment, inventory, or audit-log issues, contact an owner immediately before deleting or changing records. Keep screenshots and the exact date/time of the issue.

D Company ERP Privacy Policy

Effective date: 19 June 2026

D Company ERP is a private business operations app for D Company staff and owners. It is used for POS, inventory, finance, staff operations, reporting, audit logs, and related cafe/gaming-lounge workflows.

Brutally honest summary:

This app is not a consumer social app. It stores business records needed to run D Company. Do not enter customer personal data unless it is required for billing, loyalty, delivery, support, or legal/accounting records.

Information We Process

  • Staff account information such as name, email, role, login status, and access permissions.
  • Operational records such as orders, invoices, shifts, tables, kitchen tickets, inventory, suppliers, expenses, and reports.
  • Customer information only when entered by staff, such as name, phone number, membership details, delivery/contact notes, or purchase history.
  • Audit records showing who performed an action, what changed, and the date/time of the action.
  • Technical data required for security and reliability, such as authentication tokens, terminal IDs, and error/status logs.

How We Use Information

  • To run POS, billing, inventory, accounting, GST/reporting, staff access, and operational controls.
  • To protect the business with role-based permissions and audit logs.
  • To prepare reports, summaries, compliance records, and owner alerts.
  • To troubleshoot, secure, and improve the ERP system.

Sharing

D Company does not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers or authorities when required to operate the business, process payments, maintain accounts, comply with law, or respond to lawful requests. Examples include hosting providers, payment providers, accounting/tax professionals, and government portals where legally required.

Storage and Security

The app uses authenticated access, role-based permissions, encrypted HTTPS connections, hashed passwords, and audit logs. Some limited data, such as login tokens, terminal IDs, and offline POS data, may be stored on the device to keep the ERP usable during normal operations. Device access should be protected with a passcode or biometric lock.

Retention

Business records may be retained as long as needed for D Company operations, accounting, GST/tax compliance, legal obligations, dispute handling, backups, and audit history.

Children

The app is intended for D Company staff and owners. It is not directed to children.

Your Choices

Staff should contact an owner/admin to correct account information or remove unnecessary customer details. Some transaction and audit records may need to be retained for legal, accounting, or security reasons.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the D Company owner/admin through the support address configured for your deployment.
